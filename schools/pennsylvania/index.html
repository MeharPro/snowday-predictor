‚ùì Pennsylvania Snow Day FAQs

Will Philadelphia schools close tomorrow?

Use our predictor with your Philly ZIP (19xxx). SDP announces by 5:30 AM via website, app, and 6ABC/NBC10/CBS3/Fox29.

Does Erie get more snow than Philly?

Yes! Erie averages 100+ inches/year (lake effect) vs Philadelphia's 20-25 inches. Erie schools close much more frequently.

When do Pittsburgh schools announce?

Pittsburgh Public Schools announces by 6:00 AM via their website, app, and local news (KDKA, WTAE, WPXI).

Do suburban districts follow Philadelphia?

No! Each PA district decides independently. Lower Merion, Central Bucks, and other suburban districts make their own calls.
