‚ùì Massachusetts Snow Day FAQs

Will Boston schools be closed tomorrow?

Use our predictor with your Boston ZIP code (021xx). BPS announces by 5:00 AM via bostonpublicschools.org, BPS app, and WBZ/WCVB/NBC10.

When does Boston announce closures?

Boston Public Schools typically announces by 5:00 AM. Check the BPS website, download the BPS app, or text "BOSTONPS" to 888-777 for alerts.

How do nor'easters affect MA schools?

Nor'easters can dump 1-2+ feet of snow. Most districts close preemptively. Coastal areas face flooding risks that can trigger closures even with less snow.

Does Worcester get more snow than Boston?

Yes! Worcester is at higher elevation and typically gets more snow than coastal Boston. Central MA closures are often more frequent.

Are suburban districts independent from Boston?

Yes! Each MA city/town has its own school district. Cambridge, Brookline, Newton all make independent decisions.

What about Cape Cod schools?

Cape Cod schools (Barnstable, Dennis-Yarmouth, etc.) face unique coastal storm risks. Nor'easters can cause flooding and power outages beyond just snow.
