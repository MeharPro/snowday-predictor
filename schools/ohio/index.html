Will Cleveland schools close tomorrow?

Use our predictor with your Cleveland ZIP (44xxx). CMSD announces via website, app, and local news (WEWS, WKYC, Fox 8).

What is the Lake Erie snow belt?

Cold air over warmer Lake Erie creates intense snow bands from Cleveland to Ashtabula. Northeast Ohio can get 2+ feet while Columbus stays dry!

Does Columbus close less than Cleveland?

Yes! Columbus averages 25-30 inches/year vs Cleveland's 60+ inches. Columbus City Schools closes less frequently.

When do Ohio schools announce?

Most districts announce by 5:30-6:00 AM. Check your district's website, app, or local TV stations.
