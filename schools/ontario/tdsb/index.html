‚ùì TDSB Snow Day FAQs

Will TDSB be closed tomorrow?

Use our predictor with your Toronto postal code. TDSB announces by 6:00 AM via tdsb.on.ca, @TDSB Twitter, and CP24/CTV/Global.

When does TDSB announce closures?

TDSB typically announces by 6:00 AM. Follow @TDSB on Twitter or check tdsb.on.ca for the fastest updates.

Does TDSB close often?

No! TDSB rarely closes entirely. Toronto's TTC transit and snow removal mean schools often stay open during moderate snowfall.

Is TDSB different from TCDSB?

Yes! TDSB is public/secular. TCDSB is Catholic. They make independent closure decisions.

What about bus cancellations?

Toronto Student Transportation may cancel buses even if schools stay open. Check torontoschoolbus.org separately.

How many schools are in TDSB?

TDSB is Canada's largest school board with 582 schools: 469 elementary and 113 secondary schools serving 235,000 students.
