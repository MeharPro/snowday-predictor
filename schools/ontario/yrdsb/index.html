Will YRDSB be closed tomorrow?

Use our predictor with your York Region postal code. YRDSB announces by 6:00 AM via yrdsb.ca and @YRDSB Twitter.

Does northern York get more snow?

Often yes! Newmarket, Georgina, and East Gwillimbury typically get more snow than areas near Toronto.

Is YRDSB different from YCDSB?

Yes! YRDSB is public. YCDSB (York Catholic) is the separate Catholic board. They decide independently.
