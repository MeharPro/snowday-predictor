‚ùì Ontario Snow Day FAQs

Will Toronto schools (TDSB) be cancelled tomorrow?

Use our Snow Day Predictor to check TDSB closure probability. Enter your Toronto postal code (M prefix) for an instant prediction. TDSB announces closures by 6:00 AM.

When does PDSB announce snow days?

Peel District School Board (PDSB) announces decisions by 6:00 AM through their website, Twitter @PeelSchools, and local news. Student Transportation of Peel handles bus decisions.

Does YRDSB close when TDSB closes?

Not necessarily! Each board makes independent decisions. York Region DSB may have different closures than TDSB on the same day. Always check your specific board.

How much snow cancels school in Ontario?

It varies by board. HDSB has closed with 10cm+. Rural/northern boards may close with less. Factors: snowfall timing, wind, temperature, visibility, and road conditions.

When does HDSB announce closures?

Halton Student Transportation Services (HSTS) monitors from 4:00 AM. Decisions are typically announced by 6:30 AM via HDSB website, Twitter @HDSB, and CP24.

What about 2-hour delays in Ontario?

Some boards, particularly in Northern and rural Ontario, use 2-hour delays instead of full closures. SCDSB, TLDSB, and others may implement delayed starts during moderate weather.
