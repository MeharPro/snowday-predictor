‚ùì HDSB Snow Day FAQs

Will HDSB schools be cancelled tomorrow?

Use our Snow Day Predictor with your Halton Region postal code to check. We analyze real-time weather data including snowfall, temperature, wind speed, and visibility. HDSB announces closures by 6:30 AM via their website and Twitter @HDSB.

When does HDSB announce school closures?

Halton Student Transportation Services (HSTS) begins monitoring weather at 4:00 AM. Decisions are typically announced by 6:30 AM through the HDSB website, Twitter @HDSB, the HDSB mobile app, and local news (CP24, CTV Toronto).

How much snow does it take to cancel HDSB schools?

Based on historical data, HDSB has closed with as little as 10cm of snow when combined with other factors. Key factors: timing of snowfall (morning rush hour is critical), intensity, temperature, wind chill, visibility, and road conditions.

What is HSTS and how does it affect closures?

Halton Student Transportation Services (HSTS) manages school buses for both HDSB and HCDSB. When HSTS cancels buses, schools often close. Check hsts.on.ca for bus cancellation announcements.

Does HDSB close when HCDSB closes?

Not always! HDSB and HCDSB (Halton Catholic) make independent decisions. However, both use HSTS for buses, so if buses are cancelled, both boards are typically affected.
