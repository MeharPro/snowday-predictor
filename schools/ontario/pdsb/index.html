Will PDSB be closed tomorrow?

Use our predictor with your postal code. PDSB announces by 6:00 AM via peelschools.org and @PeelSchools Twitter.

Is PDSB different from DPCDSB?

Yes! PDSB is public/secular. DPCDSB is the separate Catholic board. They decide independently.

Does Caledon close more often?

Sometimes! Caledon is more rural with longer bus routes. Buses may be cancelled while schools stay open.
