‚ùì Michigan Snow Day FAQs

Will Detroit schools be closed tomorrow?

Use our predictor with your Detroit ZIP code (48xxx). DPSCD announces through their website, app, and local news (WXYZ, WDIV, Fox 2).

How does lake effect snow work?

Cold air over warmer Lake Michigan picks up moisture and dumps it on western MI. Grand Rapids can get 2+ feet while Detroit stays dry!

When do Michigan schools announce?

Most districts announce by 5:00-6:00 AM. Rural districts with longer bus routes may announce earlier. Check your district's app.

Does the Upper Peninsula close more often?

Yes! The UP averages 150-200+ inches of snow annually. UP schools are more accustomed to snow but still close for major storms.
